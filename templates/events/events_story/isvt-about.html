A collective of Iranian students at Virginia Tech.

The Iranian Society at Virginia Tech is a non-religious, non-political student organization aiming to promote Persian culture among Virginia Tech Community and support the Iranian community in Blacksburg and National Capital Region.

The purpose of this webpage is to serve Iranian students and anyone who is interested in Iranian society at Virginia Tech. You're more than welcome to become a member and post any personal ads and discussion here.
